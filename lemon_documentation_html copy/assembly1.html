Lemon Educational Kit:

Assembling Model 1: Lime


Lime is the simplest level available in the kit. It resembles a fish and is a perfect way to start building up your knowledge about robotics.

Lemon, is an educational kit for introductory modeling, mechanics, electronics, and programming courses, that enables learners to engage with all these concepts while enhancing their computational and design thinking skills. It consists of 3 consecutive robots that each represent a level of learning and a stage of mechanical complexity in organisms. Each stage teaches the learner how to make a biomimetic robot and it advances in difficulty with each stage, so the learners get a chance to experience all three stages, fully comprehending all the concepts necessary to make a functional robot. The simplest stage is the fish robot which is the first step of the kit, the next is a turtle, and the third is a dog. The kit consists of laser-cut kraftplex, screws, Raspberry Pi Zero, servos, motor drivers, cables, a battery holder, batteries, breadboard, and an ultrasonic sensor. It is a kit that allows learners to experience and learn about mechanics, coding, robotics, and electronics based on the workings of organisms and the help of the instructional manual of the kit. Lemon kits are not only prototyping kits but also STEM kits. When completing every stage, makers will be able to do the mathematics and kinematics calculations.
